## 🎯 **Typography Standards**

### **Font Hierarchy:**
- **Headers**: 14pt Helvetica Bold, Dark Gray (`rgb(51, 51, 51)`)
- **Subheaders**: 12pt Helvetica Bold, Black
- **Body Text**: 11pt Helvetica Normal, Black
- **Details**: 10pt Helvetica Normal, Black
- **Footer**: 8pt Helvetica Normal, Gray

### **Color Palette:**
- **Primary Green**: `rgb(22, 101, 52)` - Headers
- **Success Green**: `rgb(34, 139, 34)` - Excellent category, bullets
- **Warning Orange**: `rgb(255, 140, 0)` - Average category
- **Danger Red**: `rgb(220, 53, 69)` - Bad category, alternatives
- **Text Gray**: `rgb(51, 51, 51)` - Headers
- **Light Gray**: `rgb(100, 100, 100)` - Secondary text
- **Background Gray**: `rgb(248, 249, 250)` - Section backgrounds

## 📊 **Layout Structure**

### **Page Layout:**
- **Margins**: 20pt on all sides
- **Content Width**: Page width minus margins
- **Line Height**: Optimized for readability
- **Section Spacing**: 15pt between sections
- **Consistent Alignment**: Left-aligned text

### **Professional Tables:**
- **Header Styling**: Gray background with borders
- **Row Alternation**: White and light gray rows
- **Cell Padding**: 3pt for comfortable reading
- **Border Styling**: Light gray borders (`rgb(200, 200, 200)`)

### **Smart Page Management:**
- **Automatic Page Breaks**: When content exceeds page height
- **Consistent Headers**: Applied to all pages
- **Footer Continuity**: Page numbers and branding on every page
